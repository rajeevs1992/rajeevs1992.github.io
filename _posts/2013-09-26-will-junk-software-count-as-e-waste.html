Many of such so called projects were never used by anyone and are just piled up in my hard-disk,forcing me to create a separate directory for such E waste generated by me.And to my astonishment the directory has grown very much since I created it.


Out of the above files the projects done for the college or due to the college are listed below 

1.Books : A E book manager:As part of accreditation activities for the Department of CSE Library.

2.mp-and-mc-book : I hoped this project (book) will make much better progress,but it did not.The lead    author got busy with his academics,and that's a good enough reason.

3.Project Management System: Done in PHP,redone in Python.The PHP version was a fully working prototype and the python version helped me by serving as my mini project. The PHP version was planned for going over the college intranet,but no,ended up in my junk folder.

4.Sinfora : One of my recent projects,and one of the most challenging ones I have ever done.Demonstrated it in front of the prestigious staff of the College and was almost approved,but  remained dead for the past two months,until last week,got a call regarding hosting it. This project "may" go on air.

5.Treasurehunt: Built based on an innovative idea from my friend in EEE.Talked about it nearly 6    months ago,built it nearly 3 months ago,and now he too is preoccupied with himself.

There are a few more I don't list here.Forget those.Lets analyze these first.
