--- layout: post title: CE Amplifier Breadboard Circuit date: '2011-11-05T17:55:00.000+05:30' author: Rajeev Sreedharan tags: - Electronics modified_time: '2011-11-06T19:03:24.720+05:30' thumbnail: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EhWWL1jORXM/TrUoqbhLKAI/AAAAAAAAAJE/GqXOrFKcQr4/s72-c/fritzing.png blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2447788814762842787.post-4171991369299082392 blogger_orig_url: http://myfossblog.blogspot.com/2011/11/ce-amplifier-breadboard-circuit.html --- CE amplifier circuit is a bit complex for the breadboard.After too much of googling I found a virtual breadboard software named Fritzing(Platform : Linux with Qt).


The best feature of this software is that it generates Schematic,breadboard and PCB circuits on building any one of these.I would recommend building the circuit on breadboard and verifying it in the schematic view,as it is hard to edit the breadboard circuit.

After breadboarding you have a temporarily connected circuit that must be routed.The circuit that was automatically generated will not be legible and lacks arrangement.Move or rotate components so as to make the circuit full of straight lines only.

The next step is to route the circuit,which can be done automatically using Auto-route button or manually joining the terminals.
