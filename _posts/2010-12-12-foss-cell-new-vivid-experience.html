Foss cell is indeed a wonderful venture which is large enough to brag about.When my other schoolmates speak of the ragging they received from their seniors in college,only I have anything +ve to speak about my seniors.

The popularity and the quality of the sessions held are indeed clear from the difference in strength in our first meeting and the second.The spark we students received to the world of Linux Shell Scripting and the Inspirations from the achievements of others are priceless.The increased number of mails in the Foss cell group is another clear evidence of the success.As a result of the campaign I and many others have shifted to free software..............Last but not the least,Even this blog is posted through Ubuntu..................:-)
